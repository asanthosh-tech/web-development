Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check my result?

Enter your roll number in the search box to view your result instantly.

Can I edit my student details?

Yes. Click the Edit button, update the details, and then click Update Student.

Is my data saved after refreshing the page?

Yes. The project uses Local Storage, so your data remains available even after refreshing the page.arate hostels are available for boys and girls.

What is the Pass percentage?

Around 85% of eligible students are passed every year.

What is the minimum CGPA to appear in the Top Students section?

Students with a CGPA of 9.0 or above are displayed in the Top Students section.
